About

... The Green Pantry Project!

We decided to call this the "Green Pantry Project" because we use no chemicals in our garden, and 100% of harvests go to the Johnston Food Pantry. We keep trying to find greener ways to help the garden and our community thrive. For example, one of the ways we aid the community is by providing more than 100 pounds of fresh produce to the Johnston Partnership. Contact us to get involved, and find about the Johnston Partnership by clicking 'Read More'!
